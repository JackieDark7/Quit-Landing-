Everything You Need

Tools designed for the hardest moments

Every feature was built for the moments when willpower alone isn't enough.

Crave Timer

A craving lasts 3–5 minutes. The timer counts you through it — proving each urge is temporary and you are stronger.

Box Breathing

A guided 4-4-4-4 breathing exercise with a live animated orb. Activates your parasympathetic nervous system in seconds.

Voice Journal

Record what you're feeling, then dissolve it. Watch your words break apart into particles — a ritual of letting go.

Healing Timeline

Science-backed milestones showing exactly how your body and mind are recovering — from the first hour to years down the road.

Savings Tracker

See exactly how much money you've saved — and project it forward. Watch your financial freedom grow alongside your health.

Emergency SOS

Crisis mode when cravings hit hardest. Instant access to breathing exercises and your personal "why" — in one tap. Plus a direct line to SAMHSA's free 24/7 helpline when you need a real voice.
