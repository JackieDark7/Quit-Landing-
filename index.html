When a craving hits, open the Calm section. Choose from four breathwork techniques or start a meditation. Slide to begin — and breathe your way through.

Built for the Hardest Moments

Every tool grounded in behavioral science

Designed for the moments when willpower alone isn't enough — and proven to help.

Calm Section

Four guided breathwork techniques — box breathing, resonance, 4-7-8, and physiological sigh — plus meditation with sacred geometry visualizations.

Voice & Text Journal

Record what you're feeling or write it down. Then release it — watch your words dissolve. A ritual of letting go, not holding on.

Healing Timeline

A progress ring and milestone tracker showing exactly how your body and brain are recovering — with a breathing glow animation that mirrors your healing.

Savings Tracker

See exactly how much money you've saved — daily, weekly, monthly, yearly. Watch your financial freedom grow alongside your health.

Emergency SOS

Crisis mode when cravings hit hardest. Instant access to breathwork and your personal "why" — in one tap. Plus a direct line to SAMHSA's free 24/7 helpline.

Progress Charts

An 8-week urge trend chart showing your cravings weakening over time. Visual proof that it's working — even on the hard days.
